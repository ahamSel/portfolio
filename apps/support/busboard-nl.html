How It Works

1. Find Nearby Stops

Open the app to instantly see stops near your current location.

2. Live Tracking

Watch buses move in real-time on the map to see exactly where your ride is.

3. Arrival Boards

Tap any stop to view live countdown times for upcoming buses.

Tips for Best Experience

  • Enable location services to use the "Near Me" feature effectively.
  • Tap the "Filter" icon on the map to show only specific bus routes.
  • Select a stop to focus the map and see detailed arrival info.

Privacy & Security

We respect your privacy. Location data is used only to show you relevant transit info and is never stored on our servers. We do not track your movement history.


Frequently Asked Questions

Why is a bus missing from the map?

BusBoard NL relies on data from Metrobus. Occasionally, a bus's GPS tracker may be offline or the data feed may be delayed. In these cases, scheduled times are shown instead.

Is this the official Metrobus app?

No, BusBoard NL is an independent app created to provide a modern, fast tracking experience. We are not affiliated with Metrobus Transit.

How do I switch to Dark Mode?

The app automatically matches your device's system theme. Switch your phone to Dark Mode, and BusBoard NL will follow suit.
