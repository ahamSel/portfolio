Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases. This will re-sync your device with the App Store to recognize any previous purchases.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Delete Account. Please note that this action is immediate and permanent. All your data will be erased.

Why did my generation fail?

Ensure you have a stable internet connection and are uploading clear photos. If the issue persists, please contact us for assistance.

Where can I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed directly through your Apple ID settings. You can view, cancel, or change your subscription in the App Store app under your account.
