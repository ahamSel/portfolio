BusBoard NL Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 15, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to BusBoard NL. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices regarding collection, use, and disclosure in relation to the BusBoard NL mobile application.

Data Collection

We prioritize user privacy and collect minimal data:

  • Location Data: BusBoard NL requests access to your device location to identify nearby bus stops and show your position on the map. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect identifiable usage data or track your movement history.

Data Usage

Any data accessed by the app is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To display relevant transit information based on your current location.
  • To sort bus stops by distance from you.

Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services to provide transit data:

  • Metrobus Transit: We retrieve public transit schedules and real-time vehicle positions from the Metrobus Open Data API.
  • MapLibre / OpenFreeMap: We use these services to render the map interface.

Children's Privacy

Our service is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
