PropertyCopy AI Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 20, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PropertyCopy AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your information is handled. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices regarding collection, use, and disclosure.

Data Collection

We collect information to provide and improve our services:

  • User Provided Information: When you create an account, we may collect your email address and authentication credentials via third-party providers (Google, Apple).
  • Property Data: We collect the photos you upload and the property details derived from them to generate listings.
  • Usage Data: We may collect limited data on how you use the app to improve performance and stability.

Data Usage

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of generating real estate descriptions from your photos.
  • To manage your account and subscription status.
  • To process transactions and maintain purchase history.

Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services to operate our app:

  • Firebase (Google): Used for secure authentication, database storage (Firestore), and media storage (Firebase Storage).
  • Vertex AI (Google): Used to analyze your property photos and generate descriptions. Your photos are processed securely for this purpose.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You have full control over your data:

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by going to Settings > Delete Account within the app. This action is immediate and permanent.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
