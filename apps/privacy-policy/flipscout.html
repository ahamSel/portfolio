FlipScout

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

Who we are

FlipScout is a mobile and web app for evaluating products and marketplace listings for resale potential. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the service providers we rely on to operate the app.

What this policy covers (summary)

  • We collect account information from Google Sign-In and Sign in with Apple when you create or access a FlipScout account.
  • We process product photos, listing images, barcodes, listing titles, asking prices, and related scan data so FlipScout can identify items and estimate resale value.
  • We use third-party providers including Firebase, Supabase, RevenueCat, Google Gemini, and eBay to provide authentication, storage, subscriptions, and valuation features.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Camera access is used only when you choose to capture a product photo or barcode in the app.

Information we collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account data: your email address, display name, and basic profile information returned by Google or Apple sign-in.
  • Scan and listing data: photos you capture or upload, listing image URLs, listing titles, asking prices, barcodes, product notes, and saved inventory details.
  • Support messages: any details you send us by email.

Information collected automatically

  • Subscription status: entitlement and purchase state needed to manage paid plans through Apple, Google Play, and RevenueCat.
  • Standard service logs: infrastructure providers may collect logs such as IP address, timestamps, and request metadata for reliability and security.

Permissions

  • Camera: used to capture product photos and scan barcodes when you choose those actions.
  • Photo library / media access: used when you choose an image from your device.

We do not access your camera or photos in the background, and we only use these permissions to provide the scan flows you initiate.

How we use your information

  • Provide the core service: identify items, estimate resale value, show market comps, and save scan history or inventory.
  • Authenticate your account: let you sign in and access your saved FlipScout data across devices.
  • Manage subscriptions: determine plan access, scan limits, renewals, and purchase restores.
  • Operate and secure the app: prevent abuse, debug issues, and keep the service functioning.

Third-party services and data sharing

We share data only with service providers that help us operate FlipScout. These providers may process data on our behalf:

  • Firebase for authentication and media storage.
  • Supabase for application data and account-linked scan history.
  • RevenueCat for subscription and entitlement management.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play for purchase processing.
  • Google Gemini for item identification and structured scan interpretation.
  • eBay and other marketplace data sources used to research active or sold listing data.
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers that support the backend and web app.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data retention

  • Account and saved scan data: retained while your account remains active or until you request deletion, subject to any lawful retention requirements.
  • Subscription records: retained as needed for billing, accounting, fraud prevention, and support.
  • Temporary processing data: some scan-related data may be cached briefly for performance and reliability.

International data transfers

We are based in Canada, but some of our service providers may process data in other countries, including the United States. Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your data, including authenticated access controls, encrypted network transport, and managed cloud infrastructure.

Children's privacy

FlipScout is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your choices and rights

  • Account access: you can sign in or sign out through supported identity providers in the app.
  • Permissions: you can disable camera or photo access in device settings at any time, but some scan features may stop working.
  • Deletion requests: email us if you want your account and saved FlipScout data deleted.

Account deletion

Self-serve deletion may be added later. For now, email ahamconcerns@gmail.com with the subject “Delete my account – FlipScout” from the email address linked to your FlipScout account. We may need to verify the request before completing deletion.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page.

Contact
