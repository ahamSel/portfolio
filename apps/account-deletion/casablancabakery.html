Casablanca Bakery

Account Deletion

Effective Date: August 24, 2025

You can request deletion of your Casablanca Bakery app account and personal information. Some records (like order and payment information) may be retained as required by law and for legitimate business purposes (see “What we retain”).

How to request deletion

  1. Email ahamconcerns@gmail.com with the subject line Delete my account – Casablanca Bakery.
  2. Include the following so we can verify your identity:
  • App account email (required)
  • Full name (optional)
  • Phone number on the account (if provided)
  • Most recent order number/date (optional but recommended)

Optional: If you prefer a form, we can provide a Google Form link here.

What we delete

  • Your app account (Supabase user) and profile details (name, email, phone)
  • Notification tokens associated with your device/account
  • Marketing/preference data stored by us
  • Saved payment methods (cards on file) with our payment processor (Square) will be deleted/disabled.

What we retain

  • Order records and receipts necessary for operations, taxes, and auditing (typically retained up to 7 years).
  • Payment transaction records maintained by our payment processor (Square), which we cannot delete on your behalf.
  • Limited security/audit logs for fraud prevention and platform integrity.

Where feasible, we may de-identify retained order records after they are no longer needed for active operations.

Timeline & verification

  • We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days.
  • We may verify by emailing a confirmation code/link to your account email or by cross-checking your recent order information.
  • If you have an active order, we will begin processing but may complete deletion after the order is fulfilled or canceled.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is completed.

After deletion

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • To use the app again, you’ll need to create a new account.
  • If you signed in with Google or Apple, you may also remove Casablanca Bakery’s access in your Google/Apple account settings.

Questions

For any questions about account deletion, contact us at ahamconcerns@gmail.com.
